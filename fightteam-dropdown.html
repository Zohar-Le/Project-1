Fight Team Training

For those that want to compete, or just take their fitness to the next level an train like a fighter. These classes will go over more in depth drills, and have a higher level of intensity and physicality. We simulate in fight situations to better prepare our bodies and minds for battle. Mouthguards are required as there will be contact.
