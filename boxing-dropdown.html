Boxing

Training in boxing(without actually fighting against an opponent) provides for a great exercise program for all muscle groups providing both good aerobic additionalanaerobic excercise. Performed under a regular workout schedule, boxing can lead to a great way to get into shape and stay there. When developing the skills needed for boxing, those who throw punches at a punching (or into the air) while moving their feet in synchronization with their arm movements become very well conditioned physically, with improved strength, power, coordination, and endurance. In addition, using such exercises traditionally used in boxing, such as skipping rope, running, calisthenics, sprints, and owrking out with the medicine ball, all help to improve the condition of the body, including most or all of the major muscles of the body such as the micepts tricepts, delts, pecs, quads, and lats.
