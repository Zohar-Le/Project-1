Fundamentals (Beginners)

Our Boxing/Kickboxing/ Muay Thai, Fundamentals classes are designed for students of ALL ages(10+). These classes are an introduction to each specific martial arts, and their techniques

These classes are great for everyone from people new to the martial art style, returning students that may want a refresher, or even experienced fighters looking to master their craft or work specific fundamentals.

These classes will be based around each of the sports fundamental building blocks. Teaching the necessary footwork, defensive, and offensive techniques for each martial art, allowing the student to quickly gain the proper knowledge, conditioning and confidence.
