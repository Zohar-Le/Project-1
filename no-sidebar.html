About Us

Head Coach

Mikel Finnin has been involved in the material arts scene since 2012. He he has studied under some of the most accomplished Head Coaches available in Washington, Mark Hunter of CMMA, Travis Doerge of 6 feet under MMA, and Landon Showalter of AKA, put together is a culmination of over 12 years striking experience split between Boxing, Kickboxing, and Muay Thai. Was in active competition from 2012-2019. Although he found a new passion for coaching in 2020witht he help of Brandon Thorn head coach of Mutual Combat sports and fitness facility in Mountlake Terrace WA. After finding a location Mikel decided to set out on his own and opened his Everett facility Ring Kings Combad Sports and Fitness in 2022.

Youth Athlete Sponsorship Programs

Ring Kings Combat Sports and Firtness LLC is affiliated with a local non-profit organiztion that focuses on local youth athletes whose family may have been affected finacially by Covid-19 or whose families that are already recieving benefits from the state. By working together we have programs in place to cover part or 100% of monthly tuition and associated equiment needed.

These programs are reserved for qualifying youth under the age of 18. For more information please reach out to our team at RinkKingsMMA@gmail.com
